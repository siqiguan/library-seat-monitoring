Gelman Library

Location

2130 H Street, NW, Washington DC 20052

Regular Semester Hours

Monday – Sunday 7:00 am – midnight

Description

Study rooms are open 24 hours, 7 days a week (except holidays). Library is open to students, faculty, and staff of the George Washington University, users affiliated with university and library consortia, Foggy Bottom and West End Association members, IMF/World Bank Employees, School without Walls students and staff, Summer Housing Residents, WRLC employees, and Woodrow Wilson Scholars. Ask a librarian a question online via email or IM, or schedule a research assistant appointment online or via phone.
