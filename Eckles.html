Description

Eckles Library, as part of the Gelman Library System, is a member of the Washington Research Libraries Consortium and therefore extends borrowing privileges to eligible patrons from other consortium libraries. A valid institutional ID is required for borrowing. Some library services or privileges may be restricted to GW students, faculty, and staff
