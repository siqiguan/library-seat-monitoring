Description

The privilege of borrowing library resources is not open to the general public. General borrowing privileges are extended to all GW faculty, students, staff and University hospital employees. However, some library services or privileges may be restricted to GWU Medical Center faculty, students and staff. University personnel and students not affiliated with the Medical Center must fill out a user application form at the Himmelfarb Circulation Desk in order to register for borrowing.
